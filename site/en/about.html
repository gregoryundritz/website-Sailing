Your host

Gregory, a passionate sailor

A skipper for over twenty years, I have sailed on Swiss lakes and at sea. Sailing is not just a hobby for me — it’s a way of seeing the world differently, of reading the wind, of finding one’s course.

By offering this sailing boat for rental, I want to share this access to the lake and to sailing with those who don’t yet have their own boat. Whether you’re a curious beginner or an experienced skipper looking for an exceptional stretch of water, Lake Neuchâtel has everything to surprise you.
