Exclusive offer

Skipper Pass

Free yourself from the hassles and rediscover the pure joy of sailing. The Skipper Pass offers you 5 individual days on Lake Neuchâtel, to be used as you wish throughout the year.

It’s the ideal stepping stone for new sailors who want to gain confidence after obtaining their licence, or for anyone who wants to enjoy the lake without the hassles of ownership (mooring, maintenance, fixed costs). Cast off, we’ll take care of the rest.

  • ✦ 5 individual outings — non-consecutive, to be used as you wish
  • ✦ Weekdays only — weekdays excluding public holidays
  • ✦ Subject to availability — book your dates in advance
  • ✦ Valid all year round — 5 trips to be used within the current year
  • ✦ Fuel and briefing included
